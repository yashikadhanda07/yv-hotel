About Us

A Legacy of Luxury
in the Heart of Panipat

Where heritage meets modern elegance

Nestled on the historic GT Road in Panipat, YV Royal Hotel stands as a beacon of refined luxury and warm Indian hospitality. For over 15 years, we have been the preferred destination for discerning travelers seeking an extraordinary experience.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every detail — from the intricately designed interiors to our meticulously curated services. Whether you visit for business or leisure, YV Royal Hotel promises a stay that transcends expectations.
